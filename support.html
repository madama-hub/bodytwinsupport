Need help?

We’re here to help with account, subscription, billing, and app issues. For the fastest response, email us with your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

Contact

  • General Support: support@bodytwin.ai
  • Privacy Requests: privacy@bodytwin.ai

Common topics

  • Subscription & billing: Managed by Apple through your App Store account settings.
  • Restore purchases: Open BodyTwin and tap Restore on the paywall screen.
  • Apple Health sync: Check iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → BodyTwin.
  • Account deletion: Send a request to privacy@bodytwin.ai.

Response time

We typically respond within 1–3 business days.
